What Is Body Mass Index (BMI) and What Does It Measure?

By Maria

Body mass index (BMI) is a calculation that is sometimes used in healthcare settings as an indirect method to determine a person's body weight category. This BMI calculator can help you learn what this measurement means and how it relates to your health and fitness.

Calculate your BMI index

Note: BMI is not used for muscle builders, long distance athletes, pregnant women, the elderly or young children. This is because BMI does not take into account whether the weight is carried as muscle or fat, just the number. Those with a higher muscle mass, such as athletes, may have a high BMI but not be at greater health risk. Those with a lower muscle mass, such as children who have not completed their growth or the elderly who may be losing some muscle mass may have a lower BMI
